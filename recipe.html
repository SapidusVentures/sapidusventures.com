Great-granny Alice's Maryland Crab Cakes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Maryland-caught blue crab meat (steamed)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 2 drops Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 drops hot sauce
  • 1 tbsp parsley
  • 1 tbsp OLD BAY®, or less
  • 2 tbsp (heaping) mayonnaise, or more
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ cup instant mashed potatoes, or more

Directions

Mix all ingredients except crab meat and instant potatoes. Add crab meat and fold into mixture. Add instant mashed potatoes a little at a time until the crab cakes are not runny, but not too dry. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or as long as overnight.

Bake at 350° for 5 minutes, then broil until tops are brown. Serve immediately.
